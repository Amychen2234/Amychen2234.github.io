How you value a company

Methods: 1) Discounted cash flow 2) Public comparables 3) Acquisition comparables 4) Merger analysis 5) Leverage Buyout 6) Others

Public comparables: typically comparision with similar companies at a specific time period. Stock/share price=PE multiple * EPS (value driver).Other multiples such as enterprise value as a multipe of LTM sales, LTM EBITDA, and LTM EBIT can also provide insights into how much investors are valuing the company's operational performance.

Public comparables calculation steps: 1)peer group comps 2) calculate market value and enterprise value 3) calculate financial performance statistics 4) calculate multiples 5) analyze results and derive a range
